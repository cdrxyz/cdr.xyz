## Overview

A private chauffeured transportation service was operating without any digital presence — no website, no branded email, no professional stationery. All bookings came through word of mouth and a personal phone number. For a service selling premium experiences, the lack of a professional public face was leaving money on the table and limiting growth.

Cedar Labs was engaged to build the complete digital presence from the ground up. The owner needed everything done at once — a website that would convey professionalism and make booking easy, business cards worth handing out, and a custom domain with email that would look legitimate on every interaction.

Over the engagement, Cedar Labs delivered:

- a modern, mobile-friendly landing page showcasing transportation services, service area, and the vehicle fleet — designed to convert curious visitors into booked rides
- professional business card design ready for print, matching the website's visual identity
- custom domain registration and DNS configuration
- branded email hosting setup so all client-facing communications came from the company domain
- a contact section with direct phone, email, and a Google Maps embed for the service area

The result was a cohesive, professional brand presence that transformed the business from word-of-mouth-only to discoverable online — with every touchpoint, from the website to an email signature to a business card, presenting a consistent, premium image.
